- Eliminates the horror of discovering you're trying to die
- Makes the beacon mechanic feel arbitrary instead of tragically ironic

### 1.5 Description Writing Rules

#### DO Focus On:
- **WHAT** the mod does mechanically (*"Increases fire rate by X%"*)
- **HOW** it works using nanite technology (*"Overclocks weapon assembly protocols"*)
- **WHERE** it came from in vague terms (*"Beacon transmission", "Defense reward", "Salvaged tech"*)
- **EFFECTS** on gameplay (*"Trade accuracy for raw damage output"*)

#### DO NOT Explain:
- **WHY** the world exists (*No mentions of tests, challenges, terraforming*)
- **WHO** created specific technology (*No HIOX-One/Estel, no Earth humans*)
- **WHEN** things happened (*No ancient history, no timeline references*)
- **THE TRUTH** about beacons (*No hints they're traps*)

#### Tone Guidelines:
- **Keep mysterious** about the world ("*Strange mechanical sun*", "*Unknown nanite origin*")
- **Stay present-focused** ("*Current threat*", "*Immediate survival*")
- **Avoid nostalgia** ("*Lost civilization*", "*Forgotten creator*")
- **Emphasize utility** ("*Combat advantage*", "*Tactical superiority*")
